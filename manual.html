Manual Garden & graves

You control the game with a white garden creature thats based on Pacman.

Your goal is to grab all the apples as fast as possible and while doing so, there are also ghosts that try to get you. If they touch you, you lose the game.

You can pick up special golden apples and you'll be able to eat those ghosts.

the controls are as followed:

Up arrow, moves up.

Down arrow, moves down.

Arrow to the right, goes to the right side.

Arrow left, goes to the left side.
